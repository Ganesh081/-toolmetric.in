Frequently Asked Questions

How does the screen color picker work?

The screen picker uses the EyeDropper API (when available) to select colors from anywhere on your screen. Click the button and then click on any color on your screen.

What's the difference between RGB and HSL?

RGB represents colors as Red, Green, and Blue values, while HSL represents them as Hue, Saturation, and Lightness. HSL is often more intuitive for human color perception.

Can I save my color palettes?

Yes! You can add colors to your palette and export them as text. The palette is stored in your browser's local storage.

What is CMYK used for?

CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Key/Black) is primarily used in printing and design for print materials.
