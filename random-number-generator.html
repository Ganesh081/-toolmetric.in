Frequently Asked Questions

How random are these numbers?

Our random number generator uses cryptographically secure methods to ensure true randomness, making it suitable for most applications including games and statistical sampling.

What's the difference between unique and non-unique numbers?

Unique numbers ensure that no number is repeated in the generated set, while non-unique allows duplicates. The unique option is only available when the range is large enough.

Can I generate negative numbers?

Yes! Simply set a negative minimum value in the range. For example, set minimum to -100 and maximum to 100 to generate numbers between -100 and 100.

What's the maximum quantity I can generate?

You can generate up to 1000 numbers at once. For larger quantities, generate multiple batches and combine them.
