Frequently Asked Questions

What is simple interest?

Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount, not on accumulated interest. The formula is: Interest = Principal × Rate × Time.

How is simple interest different from compound interest?

Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal, while compound interest is calculated on both the principal and accumulated interest.

What time units can I use?

You can use years, months, or days. The calculator automatically converts the time to years for the calculation.

Can I calculate any variable in the formula?

Yes! You can calculate interest, principal, rate, or time by entering the other three known values.
