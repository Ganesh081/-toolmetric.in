Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between total characters and characters without spaces?

Total characters include all letters, numbers, spaces, and punctuation. Characters without spaces exclude space characters, which is useful for platforms that don't count spaces toward character limits.

How accurate is the character counter?

Our character counter is 100% accurate and counts every character including letters, numbers, spaces, punctuation, and special characters.

Can I use this for SEO purposes?

Yes! This tool is perfect for checking SEO meta descriptions (recommended 150-160 characters) and title tags (recommended 50-60 characters).

Does this work for different languages?

Yes, the character counter works with all languages and character sets, including Unicode characters and emojis.
