Text Cleaning Options Explained

Remove Extra Spaces

Converts multiple consecutive spaces into a single space. Essential for cleaning up text with inconsistent spacing.

"Hello World" → "Hello World"

Trim Leading/Trailing Spaces

Removes spaces at the beginning and end of each line. Perfect for cleaning up indented or poorly formatted text.

" Hello World " → "Hello World"

Remove Line Breaks

Eliminates all line breaks, converting text to a single continuous line. Useful for creating paragraph text.

"Hello\nWorld" → "Hello World"

Remove Extra Line Breaks

Keeps single line breaks but removes multiple consecutive line breaks. Maintains paragraph structure.

"Hello\n\n\nWorld" → "Hello\nWorld"

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between "Remove Line Breaks" and "Remove Extra Line Breaks"?

"Remove Line Breaks" eliminates all line breaks, making text a single line. "Remove Extra Line Breaks" keeps single line breaks but removes multiple consecutive ones, preserving paragraph structure.

Will this tool affect my text content?

The tool only removes spaces, tabs, and line breaks. It doesn't change words, punctuation, or the actual content of your text.

Can I undo the changes?

Yes! Use the "Swap Input/Output" button to restore your original text, or keep a copy of the original before cleaning.

Is there a limit to how much text I can clean?

There's no strict limit. The tool works with text of any length, though very large documents may take slightly longer to process.
