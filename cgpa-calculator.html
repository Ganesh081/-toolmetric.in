How to Use CGPA Calculator

  1. Select your grading system (10-point, 4-point, or 5-point scale)
  2. Enter the number of semesters you want to calculate
  3. For each semester, enter your SGPA/CGPA and total credits
  4. Click "Calculate CGPA" to get your cumulative result
  5. View your CGPA, equivalent percentage, and grade classification

Grade Classification (10-Point System)

  • 9.0 - 10.0: Outstanding (O)
  • 8.0 - 8.9: Excellent (A+)
  • 7.0 - 7.9: Very Good (A)
  • 6.0 - 6.9: Good (B+)
  • 5.0 - 5.9: Average (B)
  • 4.0 - 4.9: Pass (C)
  • Below 4.0: Fail (F)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of grade points obtained in all subjects excluding additional subjects as per Scheme of studies.

How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA is calculated by dividing the sum of grade points multiplied by credits for all subjects by the total number of credits.

How to convert CGPA to percentage?

For most engineering universities, Percentage = CGPA × 10. However, some universities use different conversion formulas.
