How to Use Attendance Calculator

  1. Enter the total number of classes conducted so far
  2. Enter the number of classes you have attended
  3. Select your required attendance percentage (usually 75%)
  4. Click "Calculate Attendance" to see your current status
  5. Use the planning calculator to plan for upcoming classes

Attendance Guidelines

  • 75% Attendance: Minimum requirement for most universities
  • 80-85% Attendance: Good standing for academic activities
  • 90%+ Attendance: Excellent attendance record
  • Below 75%: May lead to detention from exams

Tips for Maintaining Good Attendance

  • Track your attendance regularly using this calculator
  • Plan your leaves and absences in advance
  • Make up for missed classes when possible
  • Communicate with faculty about unavoidable absences

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum attendance required?

Most universities require a minimum of 75% attendance to be eligible to appear in examinations. However, some institutions may require higher percentages.

How is attendance percentage calculated?

Attendance Percentage = (Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes Conducted) × 100

Can I calculate future attendance requirements?

Yes! Use our attendance planning calculator to determine how many classes you need to attend to reach your target percentage.

What happens if my attendance is below 75%?

You may be detained from appearing in examinations. It's important to maintain good attendance throughout the semester.
