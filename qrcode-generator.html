Frequently Asked Questions

What data can I encode in a QR code?

You can encode URLs, plain text, WiFi credentials, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, and more. The maximum data capacity depends on the error correction level.

What is error correction?

Error correction allows QR codes to be scanned even if partially damaged. Higher levels provide more redundancy but reduce data capacity.

Can I customize QR code colors?

Yes! You can change both foreground and background colors. However, ensure there's enough contrast for reliable scanning.

What's the difference between PNG and SVG?

PNG is a raster image format good for web use, while SVG is a vector format that can be scaled without losing quality.
