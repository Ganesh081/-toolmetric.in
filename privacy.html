Privacy Policy

At ToolMetric, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free online tools. We believe in transparency and giving you complete control over your data. This policy applies to all tools and services offered on the ToolMetric website.

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled responsibly. Here's our promise:

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal information
  • Local Processing: All tools work entirely in your browser
  • No Tracking: We don't use analytics or tracking scripts
  • No Cookies: We don't use cookies for tracking purposes
  • No Registration: All tools are available without creating an account

📄 Information We Don't Collect

ToolMetric is designed to be privacy-first. Here's what we DON'T collect:

🚫 Personal Information

We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers.

🚫 Usage Data

We don't track which tools you use, how often, or your usage patterns.

🚫 Files & Documents

Your files and documents never leave your browser or device.

🚫 Location Data

We don't access or store your geographic location information.

🛠️ How Our Tools Work

Understanding our technical approach helps you trust our privacy commitment:

Client-Side Processing

All our tools use JavaScript running directly in your web browser. This means:

  • Your data never travels over the internet
  • No server processing or storage
  • Instant results without network delays
  • Complete offline functionality once loaded

📊 CGPA Calculator

Calculations happen in your browser. No grades or academic data are stored.

📝 Word Counter

Your text is processed locally. No content is saved or transmitted.

🔐 Password Generator

Passwords are generated using browser's crypto API. Nothing is stored.

📄 PDF Tools

PDF processing happens locally. Your documents never leave your device.

🌐 Browser Information

Like all websites, your browser automatically sends basic technical information:

What Your Browser Sends Automatically:

HTTP Headers:

Browser type, language, and basic connection info

IP Address:

Your public IP (needed for internet communication)

Standard Logs:

Basic access logs for server maintenance

No Personal Data:

We don't link this to your identity

📝 Contact Form Information

If you choose to contact us through our contact form:

  • Voluntary: Providing information is completely optional
  • Minimal Required: We only ask for what's necessary to respond
  • Used Only for Response: Information is used solely to reply to your inquiry
  • Not Shared: Your contact information is never sold or shared with third parties
  • Temporary Storage: Messages are stored only until we respond, then deleted

🍪 Cookies and Local Storage

Our approach to browser storage:

🍪 Cookies

We don't use tracking cookies. Any cookies used are essential for basic functionality (like remembering your preferences) and are minimal.

💾 Local Storage

Optional local storage only. We may store preferences (like theme choice) locally in your browser with your consent.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our privacy commitment extends to third-party services:

  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • No Advertising: We don't display third-party ads or tracking pixels
  • No Social Widgets: We don't embed social media tracking widgets
  • Minimal Dependencies: We use only essential, privacy-respecting services

👤 Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data when using ToolMetric:

🔍 Access

You can access and review any information you've provided to us at any time.

🗑️ Deletion

You can request deletion of any personal information you've provided.

🚫 Opt-out

You can choose not to provide information and still use all our tools.

📞 Contact

You can contact us with any privacy concerns or questions.

🛡️ Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Coding: We follow security best practices in our development
  • Regular Updates: We keep our tools updated with security patches
  • Minimal Data: We collect only what's absolutely necessary
  • Privacy by Design: Privacy is built into our tools from the ground up

👶 Children's Privacy

ToolMetric is family-friendly and doesn't collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our tools are educational and safe for all ages.

📅 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated date
  • Clearly marked when significant changes occur
  • Implemented with respect for existing users
  • Always designed to increase privacy protection
