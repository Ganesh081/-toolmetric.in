Tips for Professional Page Numbering

📐 Position Matters

Bottom center is most common for books, while bottom right works well for reports. Consider your document type and audience.

🎨 Keep it Simple

Use standard fonts like Times New Roman or Helvetica with 10-12pt size for professional appearance.

📄 Consider Page Ranges

Use Roman numerals for front matter and Arabic numbers for main content in formal documents.

🔍 Test First

Always preview your numbering on a sample page before applying to the entire document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF data secure?

Yes! All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.

What PDF formats are supported?

Our tool works with standard PDF files. Password-protected PDFs may not be supported.

Can I add different number formats to different pages?

Currently, the tool applies one format to selected pages. For mixed formats, you may need to process the document in sections.

Is there a file size limit?

The recommended maximum file size is 50MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.
