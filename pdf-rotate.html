How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Upload your PDF file by clicking the upload area or dragging and dropping
  2. Select rotation angle (90°, 180°, 270°, or custom)
  3. Choose which pages to rotate (all, range, odd/even, or custom)
  4. Preview your selection if needed
  5. Click "Rotate Pages" to process your PDF
  6. Download the rotated PDF document

Tips for PDF Rotation

📋 Check All Pages

Always review all pages before rotating, as some documents may have mixed orientations intentionally.

🎯 Use Page Ranges

For documents with only some pages needing rotation, use page ranges to avoid affecting correctly oriented pages.

🔄 Test First

Test with a single page first to ensure the rotation direction is correct before applying to multiple pages.

💾 Save Backup

Keep a copy of your original PDF before making rotations, especially for important documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF data secure?

Yes! All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.

Can I rotate pages individually?

Yes! You can select individual pages, page ranges, or use odd/even page selection for precise control.

What if I rotate pages the wrong way?

Simply rotate again in the opposite direction or use the reset option. Keep your original file as a backup.

Will rotation affect PDF quality?

No, rotation maintains the original quality of your PDF. Only the orientation is changed.
