How to Use Text to Speech Converter

Frequently Asked Questions

What text to speech technology is used?

We use the Web Speech API, which provides built-in speech synthesis capabilities in modern browsers for high-quality text-to-speech conversion.

Can I download the generated audio?

Yes! You can download the generated speech as an audio file for use in presentations, videos, or other applications.

What languages are supported?

We support over 10 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, and Chinese.

How can I adjust the voice settings?

You can adjust speech speed (0.5x to 2x), pitch (0.5 to 2), and volume (0 to 1) using the slider controls.
