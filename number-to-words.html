Frequently Asked Questions

What numbers can this converter handle?

Our converter can handle integers, decimals, and very large numbers up to quadrillions and beyond, with proper formatting and spelling.

How are decimals converted?

Decimals can be converted as "point" (e.g., point five), "and" (e.g., and five tenths), or as fractions (e.g., 5/10).

What currencies are supported?

We support US Dollars, Euros, British Pounds, and Indian Rupees with proper currency names and formatting.

Why include "and" in numbers?

Including "and" (e.g., "one hundred and one") follows traditional British English grammar and is often used in formal documents.
