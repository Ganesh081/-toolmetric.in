Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my message?

We typically respond to emails and form submissions within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize faster responses.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! We provide technical support for all our tools. If you're experiencing issues, please include detailed information about your browser, device, and the specific problem.

Can I request a custom tool?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new tool ideas. If you have a specific need, let us know and we'll consider it for future development.

How do you use my feedback?

Your feedback is invaluable! We use it to fix bugs, improve existing tools, and prioritize new features based on what users need most.
