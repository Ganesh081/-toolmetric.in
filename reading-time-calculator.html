Frequently Asked Questions

What is WPM?

WPM stands for Words Per Minute, which is the standard measure of reading speed. The average adult reads at about 200-250 WPM.

How accurate are reading time estimates?

Reading time estimates are based on average speeds and can vary based on text complexity, reader skill, and content familiarity.

What affects reading speed?

Reading speed is affected by text complexity, reader's vocabulary, reading purpose, content familiarity, and environmental factors.

Why calculate speaking time?

Speaking time helps presenters plan speeches, podcasts, and video content by estimating how long it will take to deliver content.
